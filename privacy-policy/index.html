3. What Information We Gather​

(a) Personal Information. “Personal Information” means individually identifiable information about an individual collected from that individual by Apito via the Apito Console or by other means (for example, by Your voluntarily submitting Personal Information to Us via the completion of a registration form or in an email You send to Us). The types of Personal Information that We may request include, without limitation, Your name, age, employer, job title, mailing address, email address, and telephone number. Where there are links between the Apito Console and third party sites or social networks, We may collect account information for accounts You use on those third party sites or social networks. In some instances, We may also collect the geo-location data of Your mobile or another device, including, without limitation, GPS-based, Wi-Fi-based, or cell-based location information. Even though We don’t view them as “Personal Information.” We may also collect Your internet protocol (IP) address, MAC address, or other machine or network identifiers as You access the Apito Console. Payment card and financial institution information is collected through our third-party vendors or service providers. We do not store credit card or financial institution information.

We require registration in order for You to use the Apito Console. As part of the registration process, You will be required to provide Us with Personal Information. Some of the Personal Information that We ask You to provide is mandatory and some are voluntary. If You do not provide the mandatory information, You may not be able to use the Apito Console or Your use may be limited to only certain portions. We reserve the right to change which parts of the Website require registration, and which do not, as We modify and update the Apito Console.

(b) Accessing and Updating Your Personal Information. You may update Your Personal Information via the Apito Console. If You should become aware that Your Personal Information with Apito is not complete and accurate, You must promptly update Your Personal Information via Your account. It is Your obligation to ensure that Your Personal Information is kept up to date. We may backup or replicate Personal Information stored through the Apito Console for disaster recovery or other purposes. Updates to your Personal Information will not necessarily be reflected in such backups or replicated data when You make those updates. In the event that any Personal Information is shared with third parties (as permitted in this Privacy Policy), updates will not be reflected in the data stored by those third parties unless or until they request an update from Apito and Apito provides that update (once again, where permitted by this Privacy Policy).

(c) Online Tracking. We, and certain service providers operating on Our behalf, collect information about Your Activity on the Apito Console and applications using tracking technologies such as cookies. Such technologies are widely used in order to make websites work, or work more efficiently, and to help Us better understand how people use the Apito Console. In addition, We may utilize third-party advertising arrangements that involve the use of cookies or web bugs, or similar technologies to help ensure that any advertising delivered to You by us or third parties is relevant to Your interests. Apito and its designees may use these technologies when You interact with the Apito Console. If You disable these technologies in Your browser, the Apito Console may not operate correctly and You may not be able to use certain of its features.

(d) Adult Audiences Only. The Apito Console is intended to be used only by persons over 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect Personal Information or other information from or about children. You agree to notify Us immediately in writing via Your Apito account by e-mail to support@apito.io, if You become aware that We have received information from or about any such individuals.

In addition, the Apito Console may contain links or other devices that take You to other websites that are also not within Our control or pull content from such websites for display on the Apito Console.
