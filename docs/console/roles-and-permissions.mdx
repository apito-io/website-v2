Apito roles and permission system is very versatile. Roles are very important part of your api. It controls how your API will operate.
Since your `API Secrets` depends on Roles you have to be careful while creating your roles.

## Creating a Role

For this, Go to Settings, then click on Roles, From the right side, click on the Create Role button. Enter a Role name and select the desired Permissions that you want
to give to this particular role.
